YOLO

creator

Developed with the goal of creating a year-long video, YOLO contains one birthday video for every day of the year (including Leap Day). Each video was systematically sourced from youTube using a series of creative contstraints about the query results, and stitched together in 10-second increments. The videos date from 1948-present, and the age of the subjects range from birth to 106 years.
