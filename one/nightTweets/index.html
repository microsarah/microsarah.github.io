#nighttweets

creator

#nightTweets is a platform that allows me to tweet in my sleep: exploring the concept of outsourcing social media to your sleeping self in order to be more active online. Every five minutes, it analyzes my sleep cycle data and takes a different action on twitter mapped to the stage of sleep I'm experiencing (light sleep, deep sleep, or REM) using a vocabulary comprised of my twitter archive. #nightTweets runs in Processing using the Twitter4J + Rita libraries, and uses data from the Zeo sleep tracker.
