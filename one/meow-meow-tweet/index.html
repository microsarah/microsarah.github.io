Meow Meow Tweet

visual design, identity

Meow Meow Tweet is a NY-based organic skincare company run by the fantastic Tara Pelletier and Jeff Kurosaki. Much like "meow meow" "tweet" is a conversation between two animals, Tara and Jeff's collaboration style is a conversation between two artists. The energetic packaging expresses who they are as a team, and plays on their animal illustrations featured in the design.
