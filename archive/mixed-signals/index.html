M1X3D 5IGNALS 🔮

M1X3D 5IGNALS is a large-scale video installation designed for the 120' x 11' screen at the beautiful IAC building in NYC. The concept finds beauty in the confusion and frustration of virtual communication. Created using Quartz Composer, AfterEffects, Final Cut + openFrameworks.

Music by M.I.A, Nu Shooz, Ptom Bjork; arrangement by Winslow Porter.
