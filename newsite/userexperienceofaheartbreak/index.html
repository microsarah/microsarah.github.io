User Experience of a Heartbreak 💔

interaction designer

about

User Experience of a Heartbreak is a series of animations documenting the ways in which our use of and feelings toward technology might change during a breakup, and how couples can remain digitally intertwined after separating. It questions the inhumane nature of algorithms and their overwhelming presence in our everyday lives & interactions.

"We have parted. Our ties are severed. But the internet remembers us."
